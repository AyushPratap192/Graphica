Explore East India

Discover the serene beauty and rich culture of Northeast India's hidden gems.

Kaziranga National Park, Assam

Kaziranga National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its population of the endangered one-horned rhinoceros. The park is also home to a variety of wildlife including tigers, elephants, and exotic bird species, making it a paradise for nature lovers.

Tawang Monastery, Arunachal Pradesh

Tawang Monastery is the largest monastery in India and an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Located at an altitude of over 10,000 feet, it offers stunning views of the Himalayas and is a center of Tibetan Buddhist culture.
