Popular Destinations

Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country", Kerala is famous for its backwaters, beaches, and lush greenery.

Tamil Nadu

Home to magnificent temples, Tamil Nadu offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Karnataka

With its rich history and natural beauty, Karnataka boasts vibrant cities and ancient ruins.

Andhra Pradesh

A land of rich culture, Andhra Pradesh is known for its heritage sites and spicy cuisine.

Top Attractions

Meenakshi Temple, Madurai

An architectural marvel, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and is known for its intricate carvings.

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is famous for its stunning ruins and boulder-strewn landscapes.

Cultural Highlights

South India is rich in traditions, festivals, and art forms, including classical dance, music, and culinary diversity.

Explore the vibrant festivals like Pongal and Onam that reflect the region's cultural heritage.
